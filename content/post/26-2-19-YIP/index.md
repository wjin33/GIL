Dr. Wencheng Jin received the prestigious [Young Investigator Program (YIP)](https://www.onr.navy.mil/2026-young-investigators) award from the Office of Naval Research. Please see the story shared by the Texas A&M College of Engineering [https://news.engineering.tamu.edu/news/2026/04/08/new-model-seeks-to-predict-costal-changes-in-real-time/](https://news.engineering.tamu.edu/news/2026/04/08/new-model-seeks-to-predict-costal-changes-in-real-time/)

<!--more-->
In this project, Dr. Jin and his team will explore GPU, learned correction and interpolation, and emulators to accelerate XBeach for real-time forecasting of coastal hydro-morphodynamics during extreme events.
