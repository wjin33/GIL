Dr. Wencheng Jin received the prestigious [Young Investigator Program (YIP)](https://www.onr.navy.mil/2026-young-investigators) award from the Office of Naval Research.

<!--more-->
In this project, Dr. Jin and his team will explore GPU, learned correction and interpolation, and emulators to accelerate XBeach for real-time forecasting of coastal hydro-morphodynamics during extreme events.
